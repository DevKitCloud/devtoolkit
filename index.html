About UUID v4

UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) v4 uses random numbers to generate a 128-bit identifier formatted as xxxxxxxx-xxxx-4xxx-yxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx.

The probability of a collision is near zero — making UUIDs safe to generate client-side without a central registry. Commonly used as primary keys in databases, request IDs, and session tokens.
